What's New?
  • National Science Day

    National Science Day is celebrated in India on 28 February each year to mark the discovery of the Raman effect by Indian physicist Sir C. V. Raman on 28 February 1928. For his discovery, Sir C.V. Raman was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1930. Guardian School Celebrates National Science Day with students presenting the models of different space activities like Solar Eclipse and Lunar Eclipse.

  • Online Classes

    With online classes, students can turn anywhere with Internet access and electricity into a classroom. It can include audio, video, text, animations, virtual training environments and live chats with professors. It's a rich learning environment with much more ﬂexibility than a traditional classroom. Guardian School has started new online school policy due to the pandemic. Online Schools were conducted in the same way as the physical schools.
